A new generation of Oyster Perpetual Submariner watches

Rolex is introducing the next generation of the Oyster Perpetual Submariner and Oyster Perpetual Submariner Date, which embody Rolex's historic connection to the underwater world. This watch now features a redesigned bracelet size and case design, slightly increased in diameter to 41 mm, whose shape is accentuated by the play of light on the sides of the case ring.

Special Diving Wathces

Without changing the appearance of the first model, the new version of the Submariner, made of Oystersteel, attracts attention with a black dial and rotating bezel with a Cerachrom disc in the same color. The Rolesor version of the Submariner Date in yellow (Oystersteel and 18K yellow gold) combines a royal blue dial with a rotating bezel with a blue Cerachrom disc. The other two versions of the Submariner Date - one in Oystersteel steel and the other in 18K white gold - are distinguished by their original design: the dial and rotating bezel with a Cerachrom disc are different colors. In the first version, a black dial is combined with a green bezel, in the second, a black dial with a blue bezel. The new generation Submariner and Submariner Date models have an anti-reflective coating on the sapphire crystal back for optimum readability.

Calibers

The new generation Submariner and Submariner Date models are powered by calibers 3230, introduced in 2020, and automatic 3235, entirely developed and manufactured by Rolex. The special design, workmanship and innovative solutions guarantee their exceptional precision and reliability. The movements are equipped with the exclusive blue Parachrom coil, which is ten times more shock and shock resistant than a conventional coil. The power reserve of both movements is about 70 hours.

Oyster case

The benchmark for durability and reliability, the Oyster case of the presented Submariner and Submariner Date watches is water-resistant to 300 meters. The case ring is carved from a solid block of Oystersteel, a highly corrosion resistant alloy, or 18K white gold.
